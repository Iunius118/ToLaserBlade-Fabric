## [Unreleased]

## [8.0.0-beta.2]
### Added
- Add boolean property `tolaserblade:blocking` to represent condition of laser blades.
- New sound events for use with `blocks_attacks` component:
  `tolaserblade:item.laser_blade.block` and `tolaserblade:item.laser_blade_fp.block`.

### Changed
- Change laser blade model and rendering to match `blocks_attacks` component.

### Fixed
- Fix subtractive rendering of laser blade.
- Fix laser blade item properties.

# Cheatsheet
ToLaserBlade-Fabric v0.2.0.0

## Items
### Laser Blade
- ID: tolaserblade:laser_blade
- Tool Type: Sword
- Durability: Infinity
- Attack Damage: 7
- Attack Speed: 2.8
- Enchanting: Available on Enchanting Table or Anvil
- Mining Speed: 12
- Mining Level: 3

Crafting Table:

- I = Iron Ingot; D = Diamond; G = Glowstone Dust; R = Redstone
  - x, I, D;
  - I, G, I;
  - R, I, x; -> Laser Blade
- I = Iron Ingot; D = Diamond; R = Redstone
  - x, I, D;
  - I, D, I;
  - R, I, x; -> Laser Blade

Smithing Table:

- Laser Blade + Stained Glass -> Laser Blade (outer blade color changed)
- Laser Blade + Stained Glass Pane -> Laser Blade (inner blade color changed)
- Laser Blade + Carpet -> Laser Blade (grip color changed)

#### Laser Trap
- When you put a Laser Blade in a Dispenser and activate it, you can attack mobs in front of the Dispenser with the Laser Blade
- In a multiplayer game with PvP allowed, laser traps can attack players

### Laser Blade (fireproof)
- ID: tolaserblade:laser_blade_fp
- Attack Damage: 8
- Mining Level: 4

Smithing Table:

- Laser Blade + Netherite Ingot -> Laser Blade (fireproof)
